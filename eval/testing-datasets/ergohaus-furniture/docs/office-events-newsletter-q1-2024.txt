ERGOHAUS INTERNAL NEWSLETTER — Q1 2024
======================================
"The Adjustable" — Employee Bulletin, Issue #17

Prepared by: People & Culture Team
Distribution: All Ergohaus staff (Dusseldorf HQ + remote)

---

WELCOME TO 2024!

Happy New Year from the People & Culture team! We hope everyone had a restful holiday
season and is ready for what promises to be an exciting year at Ergohaus. As we kick
off Q1, here are the highlights and upcoming events you should have on your radar.

---

JANUARY HIGHLIGHTS

Lunch & Learn Series: "Posture in the Digital Age"
---------------------------------------------------
Our in-house physiotherapist, Brigitte Hofmann, is running a 45-minute session on
Thursday, 18 January at 12:30 in the Breakout Room (3rd floor). Attendance is
voluntary but highly recommended — free sandwiches provided! The session will cover
common postural issues associated with prolonged desk work, and Brigitte will
demonstrate correct chair and monitor height adjustments on our showroom models.

Table Football League — Sign-Up Open!
---------------------------------------
The Q1 edition of the famous Ergohaus Foosball League is back. Sign up in pairs by
25 January via the internal portal (HR → Activities → Foosball Q1-2024). Matches
take place every Tuesday lunchtime in the canteen. Last quarter's champions, the
"Lumbar Legends" (Klaus Fischer and Tim Brandt from Sales), will be defending their
title. Good luck to all!

---

FEBRUARY HIGHLIGHTS

Valentine's Bake-Off
----------------------
Calling all bakers! Our annual Valentine's Bake-Off returns on 14 February. Bring
your best heart-themed creation to the kitchen by 10:00 and the team votes before
lunch. Last year's winner was Priya Menon (Finance) with her spectacular cardamom
rose cake. Prizes include a spa voucher and, naturally, an Ergohaus branded tote bag.

New Joiner Welcome: Marketing Expansion
-----------------------------------------
We are thrilled to welcome three new colleagues to our growing Marketing team in
February: Sofia Zanetti (Brand & Social), David Okonkwo (Digital Performance), and
Hanna Riedel (Content Strategy). Please make them feel at home — their first week
includes a full product tour of our showroom floor. A welcome lunch is planned for
Friday, 9 February in the Alte Mensa around the corner from HQ.

---

MARCH HIGHLIGHTS

Spring Clean — Office Sustainability Drive
-------------------------------------------
In March we are partnering with GreenOffice Rhein to run a "Spring Clean" initiative.
Each team will audit their supply cupboard and electronics storage for items that can
be donated or recycled. GreenOffice will collect on 22 March. More details to follow
from your team leads.

Q1 All-Hands Meeting
-----------------------
The Q1 company-wide All-Hands is scheduled for Monday, 25 March at 09:00 in the
main conference hall (1st floor). The agenda will be shared one week prior by the
Executive team. Key topics will include the Q4 2023 financial results summary, the
2024 product roadmap preview, and a department spotlight on Customer Success.

---

  maintenance. Plan leave requests and expense submissions accordingly.
