# Ergohaus GmbH — Return & Warranty Policy

**Effective date:** 1 March 2024  
**Document version:** 2.1

---

## 1. Overview

Ergohaus GmbH ("Ergohaus", "we", "us") manufactures and sells ergonomic office furniture directly to business customers through our online catalogue and via authorised resellers. This document sets out the conditions under which customers may return products, request warranty repairs, or claim a full refund.

---

## 2. Return Window

Customers may return any Ergohaus product within **30 calendar days** of the confirmed delivery date, provided the item is unused and returned in its original packaging. Products that have been assembled and used may not be returned under the standard return policy; they may instead qualify for a warranty claim (see Section 4).

To initiate a return, customers must submit a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) request via the customer portal at **portal.ergohaus.de** or by emailing **returns@ergohaus.de** within the 30-day window. Returns received without a valid RMA number will be refused.

---

## 3. Refund Processing

Once the returned item passes inspection at our Dusseldorf warehouse, a refund will be issued to the original payment method within **10 business days**. Ergohaus covers return shipping costs only when the return is due to a manufacturing defect or a fulfilment error on our part. In all other cases, the customer bears the return shipping cost.

---

## 4. Warranty Coverage

All Ergohaus products carry a **5-year warranty** covering manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty period begins on the date of delivery. The warranty does not cover:

- Damage caused by improper assembly or use outside the stated weight capacity.
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., gas-lift cylinder fatigue after extended daily use).
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) caused after delivery.
- Modifications made by the customer or a third party.

To file a warranty claim, customers must provide proof of purchase and photographic evidence of the defect. Ergohaus will, at its discretion, repair the defective component, send a replacement part, or issue a full replacement unit.

---

## 5. Product Compatibility & Accessories

Accessory items (monitor arms, cable trays, keyboard trays) are covered by a **1-year warranty** only and are not eligible for the standard 30-day return window if they have been installed. Unopened accessories may be returned within **14 calendar days**.
