# Ergohaus GmbH — Shipping & Delivery Guide

**Last updated:** 15 January 2024

---

## 1. Shipping Regions & Carriers

Ergohaus ships to all EU member states and to the United Kingdom. Deliveries within Germany are handled by our in-house logistics partner, **Franken Spedition GmbH**. International shipments to other EU countries and to the UK are handled by **DHL Freight**.

All orders are dispatched from our Dusseldorf warehouse. The warehouse processes orders Monday through Friday; orders placed before **14:00 CET** are dispatched on the same day.

---

## 2. Delivery Timeframes

| Destination | Standard | Express |
|---|---|---|
| Germany | 2–3 business days | Next business day (order by 12:00 CET) |
| EU (non-DE) | 4–7 business days | 2–3 business days |
| United Kingdom | 5–8 business days | 3–4 business days |

Express shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee (see Section 3).

---

## 3. Shipping Costs

| Order value (EUR) | Germany | EU (non-DE) | UK |
|---|---|---|---|
| Under 200 | 12.90 | 24.90 | 34.90 |
| 200–499 | 19.90 | 34.90 | 49.90 |
| 500 and above | **Free** | 49.90 | 69.90 |

Express surcharge: +15.00 EUR (Germany), +25.00 EUR (EU), +35.00 EUR (UK) on top of the standard rate.

Orders containing sit-stand desks (EH-D400, EH-D500) incur a **desk surcharge** of 25.00 EUR regardless of order value, because desk frames require a two-person delivery service.

---

## 4. Delivery Process for Large Items

Sit-stand desks and executive chairs (EH-C300) are delivered by a two-person team. The team will carry items to the customer's ground floor or up to one flight of stairs. Upper-floor delivery beyond the first floor is available at an additional charge of **20.00 EUR per floor** above the first, booked in advance.

Customers will receive an email notification with a 3-hour delivery window the day before the scheduled delivery. If a delivery attempt fails because the customer is unavailable, a re-delivery fee of **29.00 EUR** applies.

---

## 5. Tracking

A tracking link is emailed automatically when the order is dispatched. Customers can also log in to **portal.ergohaus.de** to see live shipment status. For DHL Freight shipments, the DHL tracking number is valid on the DHL website directly.

---

## 6. Damaged or Missing Items

If items arrive visibly damaged, customers must note the damage on the delivery receipt and photograph the packaging before unpacking. Claims for damaged goods must be submitted within **48 hours** of delivery via the returns portal or by emailing **logistics@ergohaus.de**. Claims submitted more than 48 hours after delivery will not be accepted under the damaged-goods policy; they may instead be handled as warranty claims.

---

## 7. Business Account Delivery Terms

Customers with an active Ergohaus Business Account (annual spend ≥ 5,000 EUR) benefit from:

- Free standard shipping on all orders regardless of order value.
- A dedicated account manager for delivery scheduling.
- Priority access to express slots.

Contact **sales@ergohaus.de** to apply for a Business Account.
